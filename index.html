Welcome to the Valley of Pittsfield

The Pittsfield Ancient and Accepted Scottish Rite is a Lodge of Perfection serving the Masonic community of Berkshire County, Massachusetts. We are proud members of the Northern Masonic Jurisdiction and work closely with our brother Valleys throughout Massachusetts and neighboring states.

Our meetings rotate between Blue Lodge locations in North and South Berkshire County, fostering connections throughout our beautiful region.

Our Mission

Masonic Education

Advancing Masonic knowledge through the degrees and teachings of the Ancient and Accepted Scottish Rite.

Brotherhood

Building lasting bonds of friendship and mutual support among our members across Berkshire County.

Community Service

Serving our local communities and supporting charitable causes throughout the region.

Recent Updates

2023

Rechartered

After going dark around 2000, the Valley of Pittsfield was rechartered in 2023, renewing our commitment to Scottish Rite Masonry in Berkshire County.

Ongoing

Growing Membership

We continue to welcome new members and strengthen our connections with Valleys in Greenfield, Springfield, and throughout the region.
