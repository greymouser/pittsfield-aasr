Meeting Information

Our Valley meets monthly at different Blue Lodge locations throughout North and South Berkshire County. This rotating schedule allows us to maintain strong connections with local Blue Lodges while serving our membership across the region.

For specific meeting dates, times, and locations, please contact our Secretary. Due to our rotating schedule, meeting locations are confirmed on a monthly basis.

Interested in Membership?

The Scottish Rite is open to Master Masons in good standing with their Blue Lodge. If you're interested in advancing your Masonic journey through the beautiful degrees of the Ancient and Accepted Scottish Rite, we would be honored to speak with you.

Membership Requirements:

  • Must be a Master Mason in good standing
  • Must believe in a Supreme Being
  • Must be recommended by a current Scottish Rite member
  • Must complete a petition and background investigation

Regional Connections

The Valley of Pittsfield works closely with other Scottish Rite bodies throughout Massachusetts and the Northern Masonic Jurisdiction:

Valley of Greenfield
